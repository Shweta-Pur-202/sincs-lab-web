About the Lab

What we study

The mission of the SINCS group is to enable sustainable growth of the biobased economy and industries while ensuring local and global ecological sustainability. Led by PI Dr. Shweta Singh, the lab works toward a synergy between industrial and natural systems aligned with the Industrial Ecology and Sustainable Engineering vision.

An interdisciplinary group housed in the Departments of Agricultural & Biological Engineering and Environmental & Ecological Engineering at Purdue University, SINCS develops mathematical models for the physical coupling of industries with natural systems — integrating process systems engineering, macroeconomic modeling, and machine learning to inform the adoption of sustainable technologies.
